SPAN 121 — Unidad 1

Presentaciones, verbos regulares, artículos y tu perfil en línea

Objetivos de la unidad / Learning Objectives

  • Introduce yourself and others using llamarse and ser
  • Use subject pronouns correctly in spoken and written Spanish
  • Conjugate regular -ar, -er, and -ir verbs in the present tense for all persons
  • Apply definite and indefinite articles, and identify noun gender by endings and context
  • Recognize and produce classroom phrases and key learning-activity vocabulary
  • Identify and produce all sounds of the Spanish alphabet
  • Describe your daily study habits using at least five different verbs
  • Write a 75–100 word online-platform profile in Spanish
  • Record a 60–90 second Video Log introduction
Nota Cultural

The United States has the second-largest Spanish-speaking population in the world — over 41 million native speakers. Spanish is the official language of 20 countries, and in the Pacific Northwest it is visible daily on menus, road signs, and community businesses — making what you learn here immediately relevant to life in Olympia.
